Visualizing Data

(1) Wordcloud of Most Frequent Words in Airbnb Reviews (using JQCloud2)

This Wordcloud was created from the 15 most frequent words pulled directly from 'reviews.csv'. The intention was to showcase a trend in positive or negative reviews by words such as "great" or "horrible". An unforeseen side effect were the abundance of conjunctions like "and" and "but" (Also refresh the page - it's quite cool).

(2) Wordcloud of Most Frequent Words in Airbnb Reviews without Conjunctions and Pronouns


Now we can clearly see the types of reviews for Airbnb - all positive, no negative words. Perhaps all the reviews that are publically shown are positive ones.

(3) Wordcloud of Most Frequent Words in Airbnb Summaries of Houses

This wordcloud is from the summaries of the houses. Rather than only getting 15 words, I decided to get all the words. It turns out they use "San Francisco" alot, but there's also some positive words scattered around though.
